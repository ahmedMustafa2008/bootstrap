Popular destinations in Palestine

Jerusalem City
Jerusalem is distinguished by its strategic geographical location, as it is considered the heart of the Middle East and a center for the three monotheistic religions: Islam, Christianity, and Judaism. The presence of the Al-Aqsa Mosque, the Holy Church, and the Western Wall in this city makes it a sacred place for millions of people around the world.
Jericho City
Jericho is considered one of the lowest areas on the face of the earth. It contains the Dead Sea, the saltiest sea in the world, and there is the shrine of the Prophet Moses, Mount Arbaeen, and others.
Hebron City
It is located south of the city of Bethlehem. It is an ancient historical city, and it contains the Ibrahimi Mosque. It is famous for a number of industries such as vegetable canning, shoe, leather, fiber, ceramics, and glass factories. The city of Hebron is considered the main commercial center for the entire Mount Hebron region, and it is also a religious, cultural and administrative center.

Definition of some cities

Hebron city

Mon, May 25th 2020

The city of Hebron, located in the southern West Bank of Palestine, is one of the oldest historical cities in the world, dating back thousands of years. Hebron is the city that houses the Ibrahimi Mosque, one of the most prominent religious leaders in the Islamic and Jewish worlds. The Prophet Abraham, his wife Sarah, and a number of other prophets are believed to be buried here, giving the city great religious and spiritual significance.


Jenin city

Mon, May 25th 2020

Jenin is one of the Palestinian cities located in the northern West Bank. Jenin is an important urban and commercial center in the region, and is famous for its fertile lands and beautiful nature. Historically, Jenin has played a pivotal role in various historical and political events that Palestine has gone through.


Acre city

Mon, May 25th 2020

Acre is one of the oldest coastal cities in historical Palestine and is located on the eastern coast of the Mediterranean Sea. The city is distinguished by its rich and diverse history, as it was inhabited by many civilizations from the Phoenicians to the Romans and the Octagonians. Acre was known as a commercial port and an important center of commercial culture in the region.


Gaza City

Mon, May 25th 2020

Gaza City is one of the oldest cities in the world, dating back more than 4,000 years. Located on the southeastern coast of the Mediterranean Sea, Gaza is one of the most important cities in Palestine due to its strategic location and ancient history.
